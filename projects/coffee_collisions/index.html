Coffee Collisions

Collide for a (virtual) coffee with another Snowmass participant to build a new connection. We'll find you someone to talk to, so you can just think about the future of particle physics!

Participants will be matched based on shared interests, but across career stages, institutes and collaborations.

The first round of collisions are currently taking place!

In case you need inspiration, maybe try using some of our conversation prompts:

  • "What proposed future facilities are you most excited about?"
  • "What's it like to work at your institute/facility?"
  • "What questions do you hope we answer in the next 40 years?"
  • "Are you involved in any outreach projects?"
  • "What was the last paper you read?"
  • "Do you think the coffee at FNAL or CERN is better?"

We'd like to hear from you after your collision! Please send a message using our feedback form. Additionally, if you'd like, feel free to post a screenshot of your chat to social media using the hashtag #CoffeeCollisions!
